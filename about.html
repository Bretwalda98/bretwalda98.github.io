About

Bretwalda98 Engineering Tools is a small engineering software site for calculation utilities, report generation workflows and technical design tools.

The current public tool is Beam Calculator Studio, a browser-based beam calculation application intended to help engineers, students and technically competent users review beam inputs, loading, reactions, diagrams and calculation reports.

Engineering use

These tools are intended to support engineering review and learning. They do not replace competent engineering judgement, independent checking, applicable design standards or project-specific verification.
